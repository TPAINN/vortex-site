Three taps, start to saved

  1. Paste or share the link

    Copy any video URL, or hit Share → Vortex from the platform's own app. Vortex reads the title, thumbnail and length before you commit.

  2. Pick your format

    Video as MP4 up to 1080p, or audio only as MP3 320 kbps / M4A with cover art and track metadata baked in.

  3. It lands in Downloads

    A notification tracks speed and ETA while you do something else. The file appears in Downloads/Vortex, ready for any player or gallery.
